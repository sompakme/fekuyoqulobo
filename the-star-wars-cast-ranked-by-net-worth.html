The legendary Star Wars films have made a killing for the acclaimed actors and actresses who have been a part of the franchise for decades.

These performers have achieved legendary status and financial success as part of the Star Wars cast, from the groundbreaking original trilogy and subsequent films.

As revealed in this ranking, the stars who play these iconic roles have earned enormous fortunes.

From seasoned A-listers to up-and-coming stars, it gives us a look at the wildly varying paths to riches of the people who brought the "galaxy far, far away" to life in theaters.

Fasten your seatbelts, as we delve into the celestial figures that stand behind the most recognizable faces of the Force.

A Note About The Star Wars' Cast's Net Worths: Estimates of the net worths of actors are largely based on publicly available information, and may not include privately held assets and liabilities, among other factors.

Whenever possible, we review a number of sources, as well as available information about salaries and other income to arrive at estimated net worths.

10 Daisy Ridley (Rey): $8 Million

Daisy Ridley has an estimated net worth of $8 million, considered modest compared to her more experienced co-stars.

The British actress was relatively unknown before securing a place among the Star Wars cast at age 23.

Despite the immense success of the Star Wars films, Ridley's income may have been restricted by typical agreements for new actors.

Nevertheless, her rapid ascent to international stardom has already opened up profitable endorsement deals and potential future earnings.

At only 30 years old, this skilled artist's wealth is set to increase quickly.

9 John Boyega (Finn): $8 Million

John Boyega was a newcomer to the industry when he landed the role of Finn in the Star Wars sequels.

Boyega's estimated $8 million net worth comes mainly from his involvement in the highly successful trilogy.

The actor from London has also taken advantage of endorsements and more minor film roles since gaining fame.

Boyega, who is 30, has a promising net worth that could see substantial expansion as he secures more roles in significant franchises and capitalizes on his increasing worldwide fame.

8 Adam Driver (Kylo Ren): $16 Million

Adam Driver's $16 million estimated net worth reflects his versatility as an actor, spanning highly praised small films and massive blockbuster franchises such as Star Wars.

Driver's performance as the wicked Kylo Ren was widely praised for its passion and complexity.

His Star Wars experience was lucrative, but many of his riches come from collaborations with directors such as Noah Baumbach and Steven Soderbergh.

With an Oscar nomination already under his belt at 39, Driver's reputation as a strong talent places him in a position to continue commanding high earnings.

7 Mark Hamill (Luke Skywalker): $18 Million

Mark Hamill will go down in pop culture annals as the legendary Luke Skywalker.

Nevertheless, his $18 million net worth pales compared to his legendary status. The fact that Hamill had a hard time getting leading roles following the first Star Wars trilogy is likely to blame.

Before returning to reprise the role of Luke in the sequels, he started doing voice-overs. Nonetheless, residuals and convention income come from his franchise relationships.

Even if his career slowed down after he became known as Luke Skywalker, Hamill's net worth shows that his legacy has endured.

6 Carrie Fisher (Princess Leia): $25 Million

Carrie Fisher had an estimated net worth of $25 million at the time of her passing in 2016, which may seem modest given her iconic status as a Star Wars princess and successful writer.

Most of her earnings stemmed from her portrayal of the bold Leia Organa, despite only receiving $3 million for the original trilogy.

Books such as Postcards From The Edge and personal accounts of her battles with bipolar disorder also played a part.

Ultimately, Fisher's openness regarding her struggles likely affected her total income from a groundbreaking career.

5 Ewan McGregor (Obi-Wan Kenobi): $45 Million

Ewan McGregor played the youthful Obi-Wan Kenobi in the Star Wars prequels, reinvigorating the series for a new generation.

McGregor's estimated $45 million net worth comes from playing this renowned Jedi Master, and other major film roles, such as in the films Trainspotting and Moulin Rouge.

The Scottish actor's versatility, spanning indie hits and big-budget spectacles, has enabled him to earn high salaries throughout his career.

At 51, McGregor still leverages his Star Wars reputation for the Obi-Wan Kenobi Disney+ series, while taking on additional prestige projects.

4 Natalie Portman (Padmé Amidala): $90 Million

At the age of eighteen, Natalie Portman became a part of the Star Wars canon as Padmé Amidala, the protagonist of the prequel trilogy.

That legendary portrayal – and highly praised features like Black Swan, for which she received an Oscar – contributed to her estimated $90 million net worth.

At 41 years old, Portman has expertly juggled her artistic passion projects with blockbuster roles like her recurring MCU presence in the Thor films.

Natalie has amassed a substantial fortune throughout her 25-year career, largely thanks to her astute business decisions and investments.

3 Liam Neeson (Qui-Gon Jinn): $145 Million

Liam Neeson's net worth of $145 million far surpasses that of most of his Star Wars co-stars.

Even though his portrayal of Qui-Gon Jinn was short-lived, the experienced Irish actor had already gained A-list recognition through films such as Schindler's List, and thrillers like Taken.

At 70, Neeson still commands high salaries for action films which leverage his on-screen solid charisma.

His time with Star Wars is just a tiny part of a highly successful career spanning over four decades.

2 Samuel L. Jackson (Mace Windu): $250 Million

Samuel L. Jackson is the second-richest actor in the Star Wars franchise, with an estimated net worth of $250 million.

Nevertheless, his total fortune is largely influenced by his successful film career beyond the series. Portraying the powerful Jedi Mace Windu, Jackson exuded his iconic coolness in the prequels.

His wealth is primarily built on his work with directors like Quentin Tarantino and Spike Lee, and through his roles in popular films like The Avengers.

At 74, the actor still commands top salaries, due to his unique on-screen charisma, ability to excel in various genres, and enormous popularity with audiences the world over.

1 Harrison Ford (Han Solo): $300 Million

Harrison Ford personified the rebellious essence of the Star Wars universe as the roguish Han Solo.

However, he has the biggest estimated net worth among the Star Wars cast at $300 million, due to memorable parts in several successful blockbuster series.

Apart from Star Wars, Ford became a box office titan with the releases of the Indiana Jones films, as well as successful one-offs like Air Force One.

This iconic leading man, now 80, has cemented his status as one of Hollywood's top earners with decades of commanding top salaries and astute business dealings.
