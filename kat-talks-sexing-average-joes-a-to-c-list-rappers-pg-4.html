Kat Talks Sexing Average Joes &amp; A To C-list Rappers [Pg. 4]

Publish date: 2024-10-01

You don’t fuck with average Joes? 

Regular dudes just wanna… I was around a regular guy for five years… I don’t want to have a serious relationship with a regular guy because he wants to settle down. Rappers just want to live their lives. I’m not looking to be at home right now.

It’s crazy how after each video it seems like you have a new rapper trying to holler. I’d personally be scared of you.

[Laughs] Yeah. I find it funny. I think because I’m just out there it attracts guys for some crazy reason.

You’re like an odd trophy groupie.

Yeah. And they don’t want the public to know and they call up my manager like ‘I want to meet up with Kat can you introduce me?

Are these A-list MCs?

The A-list guys aren’t that into me, they just call from time to time.

Who’s an example?

I’ll save that for if something happens in the future. They haven’t done anything for me to ruin their name yet.

[Laughs] Fair enough. So you mainly deal with B and C rappers.

Yeah, the A ones try to protect themselves more.

That seems even more enticing.

I feel like they’re just scared and it’s very hard to get them to where I want [laughs]. 

Can you share the Kat Stacks approach to nailing a rapper?
