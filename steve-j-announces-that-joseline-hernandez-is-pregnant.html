Steve J Announces That Joseline Hernandez Is Pregnant

Publish date: 2024-07-22

VH1’s most ratchet entertaining couple, Stevie J and Joseline Hernandez, are expecting a baby. In an interview with V100.7’s Reggie Brown in Milwaukee, “Steebie” broke the news after being probed about the authenticity of he and Joseline’s marriage.

“Yeah it’s all real,” he said. “We got a new addition with the family too, but I don’t want to say too much, you know? We’re extending the Jordan family if you understand what I mean.”

Upon’s Brown’s reiterating of the fact that Joseline is indeed pregnant, Stevie repeated that they were extending their family and accepted the deejay’s congratulations. The two also discussed lingering rumors that Joseline had been less-than-faithful to Stevie in recent months, a claim Stevie shot down.

“They talked about Jesus, man. So who are we,” he said. “At the end of the say, when they see a real powerful, strong element in our black culture, they always try to break you down. But one thing’s for certain: the Jordans are here to stay.”
