Angela Stanford is an American professional golf player with an estimated net worth of $7 million.

Angela Stanford started her golf career while studying at Boswell High School. Her winning streak began when she won the Forth Worth Girls Championship from 1993 to 1996, 1996 Texas State 4A High School Championship, and 1996 PING Texas State Junior Championship.

In college, she attended Texas Christian University and represented the Horned Frogs in several tournaments. Stanford won nine from the said tournaments and was named as a four-time All-American. She was hailed as the 1997 Freshman of the Year and 1999 WAC Player of the Year.

Stanford turned professional following the U.S. Women's Amateur in August 2000. Her first win for the LPGA Tour occurred during the third season in 2003. She has been a member of the five U.S. Solheim Cup teams in 2003, 2007, 2009, 2011, and 2013. Meanwhile, during her career she won five LPGA Tour in 2003, 2008, 2009, and 2012.

Angela Gwen Stanford was born on November 28, 1977 in Fort Worth, Texas.
