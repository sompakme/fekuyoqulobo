Barbara Eden Wears Jeannie Costume at Life Ball With Bill Clinton

Publish date: 2024-09-03

I Dream of Jeannie fans’ wish has been granted! Barbara Eden dressed up in the 1960s sitcom’s iconic costume during the Life Ball 2013 in Vienna, Austria on Saturday, May 25.

The 78-year-old actress stepped out on the City Hall stage rocking the famous pink crop top, harem pants, and braided blonde hair. She even treated the audience with a performance of her signature head nod and wink, which magically brought out her new “Master.”

Former President Bill Clinton, 66, stepped out on the stage and greeted Eden with a big smile on his face.
