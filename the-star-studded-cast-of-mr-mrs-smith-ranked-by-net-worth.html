They traded vows under a false pretense, their love story fueled by adrenaline and assassination.

We're not talking about Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie.

This series re-imagines the iconic story with a fresh cast.

Donald Glover, the multi-talented Grammy winner and Community star, brings his swagger and comedic timing to the character of John, while Maya Erskine, known for her hilarious Hulu series PEN15, delivers a sharp and witty portrayal of Jane.

And that's just the tip of the iceberg – the series features talents like Sarah Paulson, Paul Dano, and Parker Posey, promising a kaleidoscope of personalities and performances.

From Ursula Corbero's estimated $4 million fortune to Donald Glover's $40 million, the range of wealth within the cast is as diverse as their on-screen characters.

10 Ursula Corbero

Ursula Corbero's Net Worth (Estimated) - $4 Million

Úrsula Corberó has a net worth of around $4 million.

Corberó rose to fame in her homeland of Spain with her role in the teen drama series Física O Química back in 2008. This breakthrough led to a string of successful television appearances, including 14 De Abril, La República and Isabel.

However, it was her portrayal of Tokyo in the gripping crime drama series Money Heist that launched her onto the international stage. The show’s global distribution via Netflix made her a household name worldwide.

Transitioning to Hollywood, Corberó made her debut in the superhero film Snake Eyes in 2021 and also appeared alongside Kevin Hart in Gary Gray's Lift.

Additionally, Corberó has graced numerous music videos and served as the face of brands like Tampax, Stradivarius, and Maybelline.

In 2018, she launched her eyewear line for Multiopticas, and became the global ambassador for Shiseido in 2021.

9 Parker Posey

Parker Posey's Net Worth (Estimated) - $5 Million

With a successful career spanning over two decades, several films, and an estimated net worth of $5 million, it's safe to say that Parker Posey has earned her legacy in the industry.

While she may not dominate the box office with back to back blockbusters, Posey's reign as the "Queen Of The Indies" in the mid-1990s solidified her indie cred and led to roles in over 100 films.

Early roles in films like Dazed And Confused and Party Girl established her edgy, quirky persona, while The House Of Yes and Waiting For Guffman perfected her comedic timing.

She brought her signature wit to mainstream hits like Scream 3 and Superman Returns, proving she could seamlessly blend indie charm with Hollywood budgets.

Posey's biggest paydays are likely linked to her later mainstream projects. While specific figures remain undisclosed, her role as Lex Luthor's assistant in Superman Returns was undoubtedly a lucrative one.

Additionally, her recurring role as Dr. Smith in Netflix's Lost In Space (reportedly earning her six figures per episode) undoubtedly contributed significantly to Posey's net worth.

8 Eiza González

Eiza González's Net Worth (Estimated) - $5 Million

Eiza González has parlayed her telenovela roots into a $5 million net worth, proving Hollywood dreams can have a Latin American accent.

Her journey began in 2007 with the Mexican telenovela Lola... Érase Una Vez, followed by the smash hit Amores Verdaderos in 2012.

By 2013, she set her sights on Hollywood, landing her first English-language role in the Robert Rodriguez series From Dusk Till Dawn: The Series.

While details of her paycheck remain private, it marked her entry into the lucrative world of American entertainment. Her breakout came with Edgar Wright's stylish action flick Baby Driver in 2017.

Fast-forward to 2021, and González shared the screen with Godzilla and King Kong in the blockbuster Godzilla vs. Kong, another massive payday contributor.

In addition to life in front of the camera, González has leveraged her fame into lucrative brand deals.

She served as the face of Avon's Color Trend cosmetics line and collaborated with skincare giants Asepxia and Neutrogena.

7 Ron Perlman

Ron Perlman's Net Worth (Estimated) - $6 Million

Ron Perlman's gravelly voice and imposing presence have resonated across screens for decades, earning him a ton of street cred and an estimated net worth of $6 million.

While Hellboy might be his most iconic role, Perlman's journey stretches far beyond demon horns and red paint. His breakthrough came in 1987, playing the brooding beast Vincent in Beauty And The Beast, a transformative role that landed him a Golden Globe.

But it was Guillermo del Toro's Hellboy in 2004 that cemented his status as a genre heavyweight.

His salary for the role was not disclosed, but factoring in the demanding makeup and physicality of the role, he likely received substantial compensation. Perlman reprised the role in Hellboy II: The Golden Army; the film grossed over $160 million worldwide.

Perlman also delivered a gritty seven-season run as Clay Morrow, the leader of the biker gang in FX's Sons Of Anarchy. His compensation for that role is also not publicly known, but the show's popularity likely translated to a significant boost in his wealth.

Outside his on-screen appearances, Perlman has also enjoyed a successful career in voice acting. He has done voice-over work for numerous animated series, video games, and TV commercials.

Some series he has voiced include Justice League, Batman: The Animated Series, Avatar: The Last Airbender, and The Brave And The Bold, all of which were commercially successful.

6 Wagner Moura

Wagner Moura's Net Worth (Estimated) - $10 Million

Wagner Moura has an estimated net worth of $10 million as of early 2024.

While his filmography stretches far beyond Hollywood, his impact there is undeniable. Moura's rise began in Brazilian theater and transitioned to films by 2003.

His leading roles in Carandiru and the Elite Squad franchise, where he played Captain Nascimento, elevated his status as a national superstar. In 2007, he was the antagonist of the telenovela Paraíso Tropical, being praised for his performance by both the public and the critics.

In 2013, he was cast in the American science fiction feature film Elysium, opposite Matt Damon and Jodie Foster. Elysium raked in over $286 million at the box office against a budget of $115 million.

More importantly, it helped Moura make major inroads into Hollywood.

5 Sarah Paulson

Sarah Paulson's Net Worth (Estimated) - $13 Million

With an estimated net worth of $13 million, Sarah Paulson's journey to Hollywood fame started humbly.

Early roles in American Gothic and Jack & Jill laid the groundwork, but it was Ryan Murphy's American Horror Story that catapulted her into mainstream recognition.

Playing diverse characters across nine seasons, she earned $80,000 per episode. Paulson's talent was not confined to television. Films like 12 Years A Slave and Carol highlighted her dramatic range, while Ocean's 8 and Bird Box proved her commercial appeal.

Her critically acclaimed portrayal of Marcia Clark in The People v. O. J. Simpson: American Crime Story earned her a Golden Globe and an Emmy.

In 2020, her series Ratched garnered significant attention, becoming Netflix's best original launch of the year with 48 million views in its first month.

Recognized for her sway in Hollywood, Paulson was named one of Time magazine's most influential people in 2017.

4 Alexander Skarsgård

Alexander Skarsgård's Net Worth (Estimated) - $14 Million

Born into Swedish acting royalty, Alexander Skarsgård's path was not always linear.

Despite his father's success, Skarsgård initially quit acting at 13 before serving in the Swedish Navy. He eventually returned, making his American debut in Zoolander.

His breakthrough came with the HBO series True Blood, where he commanded $275,000 per episode for his portrayal of the enigmatic vampire Eric Northman, contributing to his $14 million net worth.

Skarsgård made consistent appearances in acclaimed films like Melancholia and The Legend of Tarzan, which grossed $356.7 million.

However, it was his chilling performance as an abusive husband in Big Little Lies that truly garnered awards recognition, including a Primetime Emmy and a Golden Globe.

Since then, Skarsgård has continued to navigate between blockbusters like Godzilla vs. Kong and indies like Passing. He even ventured into television again with the chilling miniseries The Stand and the power struggle drama Succession.

His latest film, The Northman, which he also produced, demonstrates his dedication to his craft, while his horror sci-fi thriller Infinity Pool further explores his diverse range.

3 Paul Dano

Paul Dano's Net Worth (Estimated) - $14 Million

Paul Dano's Hollywood ascent hasn't been about blockbuster explosions, but nuanced performances that etch themselves into viewers' minds.

His breakout came at 17 with the raw portrayal of a troubled teen in L.I.E., setting the stage for a career built on character depth. Little Miss Sunshine brought wider recognition, with Dano playing a pivotal role in the film's quirky charm.

He then captivated audiences with his quiet complexity in There Will Be Blood, showcasing his ability to hold his own opposite acting giants like Daniel Day-Lewis.

Critical acclaim followed for his portrayals in 12 Years A Slave and Prisoners, and his transformation into the Beach Boys' Brian Wilson in Love & Mercy earned him a Golden Globe nomination.

Dano's net worth sits at an estimated $14 million, but his value extends beyond paychecks. He ventured into directing with the well-received Wildlife, further displaying his artistic vision.

On television, his chilling performance in the acclaimed dramatization Escape At Dannemora garnered him an Emmy nomination.

Recent years saw him take on iconic roles, bringing The Riddler to life in The Batman, and portraying a complex father in The Fabelmans.

Beyond acting, Dano has authored the comic book The Riddler: Year One, and graced Broadway's storied stages.

2 John Turturro

John Turturro's Net Worth (Estimated) - $14 Million

While many recognize him from blockbuster franchises like Transformers and The Batman, John Turturro's $14 million net worth stems from a rich career built on diverse, complex roles.

Turturro has appeared in over 60 films, grossing a combined $6.5 billion globally, proving his box office pull. Turturro's rise began in the independent film scene.

He garnered acclaim in Five Corners and collaborated frequently with Spike Lee, for films such as Do The Right Thing. Turturro also worked with the Coen brothers on Barton Fink, for which he won the Cannes "Best Actor" award.

He forged partnerships with Adam Sandler in Mr. Deeds, and has established himself as a character actor extraordinaire. Turturro also brought his comedic flair to Anger Management, which grossed over $196 million worldwide.

He has directed five films, including the acclaimed Romance & Cigarettes, expressing his artistic vision behind the camera.

1 Donald Glover

Donald Glover's Net Worth (Estimated) - $40 Million

Donald Glover, a true renaissance man with a net worth of $40 million, wears many hats: actor, comedian, writer, producer, director, musician, and DJ.

Known in the music world as Childish Gambino, his Hollywood story began as a writer for 30 Rock. His breakout role came in Community, followed by the critically acclaimed series Atlanta, which he created and in which he starred.

Glover's filmography is worth over $3.9 billion in global gross, and spans genres from sci-fi (The Martian) to comedy (Magic Mike XXL) to superhero flicks (Spider-Man: Homecoming) — and even the beloved Star Wars franchise (Solo: A Star Wars Story).

He also provided the voice of adult Simba in The Lion King, which grossed over $1.65 billion, making it his highest-grossing film.

His creative ventures extend beyond acting, including directing and producing the short film Guava Island, and co-creating the TV series Swarm. Music is another key facet of Glover's success.

After independent releases, he signed with Glassnote Records, dropping acclaimed albums like Camp and Because The Internet. His politically charged hit "This Is America" became a global phenomenon, garnering Grammy Awards and topping the charts.

Glover also earns significant money through endorsement deals, and was featured in Gap's 2010 holiday advertising campaign.

In partnership with Adidas, Glover re-imagined three pairs of classic Adidas sneakers, releasing them in April 2019 as the "Donald Glover Presents" line.
