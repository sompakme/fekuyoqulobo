Robin Tunney, The Mentalist Star, Is Engaged to Nicky Marmet!

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Robin Tunney has a great engagement story! The Mentalist actress, 40, tells Us Weekly exclusively that she's engaged to boyfriend Nicky Marmet. Tunney accepted Marmet's proposal on Christmas Day in Rio de Janeiro — but it was actually his second attempt at popping the question.

"He'd made an earlier attempt at the beach in Bahia, but I had what I thought was Dengue Fever and I threw up over his shoulder when he was about to take the ring out," she told Us of a recent vacation to Brazil. "Mercifully, I recovered and was healthy enough to accept the second time."

"I was shocked," she added. "I was too ill to notice anything was up in Bahia so he'd caught me completely off guard."

The actress tells Us that Marmet, an interior designer, is an "amazing man" and did a great job picking out the "beautiful rock" on her finger.
