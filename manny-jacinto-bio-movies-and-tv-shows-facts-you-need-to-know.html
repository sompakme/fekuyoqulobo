Manny Jacinto Bio, Movies and TV Shows Facts You Need to Know

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Manny Jacinto is a Philippine-born television star, exceptionally popular for his role in the 2016 Michael Schur comedy television series. The good place where he portrayed the character of Jason Mendoza. He began his acting career as Kenji in the 2012 Canadian short film, Broken Sword: Shadow of the Blade. A civil engineer educated at the University of British Columbia, he has not only made a name for himself in the Canadian entertainment industry, but has also secured a place in Hollywood.

Other notable films and television series that Manny Jacinto has credited with his talent include Supernatural, The Unauthorized Saved from the Bell Story, Dead Rising: Watchtower , and The Romeo Section . He was also cast in the action drama film, Top Gun: Loner alongside Tom Cruise and Jennifer Connelly. Read on to know more about the biography and some important facts about the amazing actor.

Manny Jacinto’s biography

The great actor was born on August 19, 1987 in the city of Manila, capital of the Philippines. He was christened Manuel Luis Jacinto by his parents, whose identity was not revealed to the media by the actor. At the age of 3 his family moved to Canada and grew up in Richmond, British Columbia, Canada. Manny Jacinto is an Asian Canadian male of Filipino descent.

He was very energetic growing up and was very active in basketball and baseball, and he even considered pursuing either of them as a career. Manny Jacinto is a graduate of Vancouver College, an independent boys’ Catholic school in the Shaughnessy neighborhood of Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Shortly after completing his high school education, he enrolled at the University of British Columbia, where he received a bachelor’s degree in applied science civil engineering.

His movies and TV shows

While trying his luck at acting during his internship, Manny Jacinto appeared in the 2012 Canadian action film alongside Andrew Fudge, Owen Kwong, Ludi Lin and Henry Mah as Kenji. Broken Sword: Shadow of the Blade. 2013 was a busy year for the young actor as he made his TV debut as Quon in an episode of the multi-award winning TV series. once upon a time . He went on to act in three films – Tele, John Apple Jack, and #Fix, and two television series – Supernatural, and Untold Stories of the ER that same year.

However, Manny gained his first notoriety with his outstanding portrayal of Wing Lei in the award-winning 2015 television series, The Romeo Section . His performance on the show was highly praised.

Although Manny Jacinto is a few years old in showbiz, he rose to prominence and gained widespread attention after playing Jason Mendoza in the critically acclaimed American comedy-drama series. The Good Place starring Kristen Bell, Ted Danson,William Jackson Harper, Jameela Jamil and D’Arcy Carden. The series, which debuted on NBC on September 26, 2016, has received many awards and nominations, including winning the 2016 Critics Choice Television Awards, 2018 AFI Television Show of the Year, and two Golden Globe nominations, including two Primetime Emmy nominations.

Manny Jacinto has also appeared in Bad Times at El Royale, Brittney Grabill: Goodnight, iZombie, Dead Rising: Watchtower and many others.
