Paul Scheer Hilariously Captions Instagrams from Beyonce, Bieber, Ariana

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Caption game strong. Funnyman Paul Scheer stopped by Us Weekly Video to help celebrities including Ariana Grande, Justin Bieber, Selena Gomez, Beyoncé and more with their Instagram captions.

The League alum, 40, clearly keeps up with his celebrity news because his captions were on point! 

“Just thinking about how I love to lick donuts,” he suggested for Ariana Grande’s May 18 snapshot, which touted her Dangerous Woman album cover art. Scheer’s caption, of course, refers to Grande’s biggest scandal: that time she decided to lick a doughnut on a counter in a Riverside, California, bakery.

“Take that, Blake Lively,” the actor suggested for Nicki Minaj’s booty-in-your-face February 2016 post, jokingly referring to Lively’s recently derided “Baby Got Back” booty joke.
