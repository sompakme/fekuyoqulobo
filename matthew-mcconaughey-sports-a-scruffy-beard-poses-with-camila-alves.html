Matthew McConaughey Sports a Scruffy Beard, Poses with Camila Alves

Publish date: 2024-07-25

A star if we’ve ever seen one!  

After nabbing the 2014 Oscar Award for his role in Dallas Buyers Club, and continuing on with more challenging gigs like 2014’s Interstellar, Matthew McConaughey received his long awaited star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame on Monday, Nov. 17, in Hollywood. The hunky actor, 45, looked smooth in a tan three-piece suit by Dolce & Gabbana, sporting a wild and scruffy beard for the event. 

McConaughey was joined by his wife of two years, Camila Alves, who looked chic in a pale blue Dolce & Gabbana dress with nude pumps.

Daughter, Vida, 4, stood out in a floral print dress featuring bell sleeves, and shimmering red shoes.

Levi, 6, wore a sleek black suit and a white shirt. Lastly, little Livingston, who turns 2 next month, sported a baggy black suit with a blue T-shirt and fun sneakers.

Along with the Oscars, Golden Globes, and other awards, McConaughey told GQ magazine of his biggest award — being a father. “It’s the only thing I’ve always wanted to be.”

“I knew when I was 8 years old,” he said. “I mean I wanted to be things like the Washington Redskins running back and all that stuff, but the one thing I knew I wanted to be was a father.”
