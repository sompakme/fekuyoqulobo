Ron Isley, born on May 21, 1941, in Cincinnati, Ohio, is an American singer, songwriter, and occasional actor. He began his singing career at a young age, showcasing his talent in church performances. In 1957, Isley and his brothers made a pivotal move to New York, where they signed a major record deal with RCA Records in 1959. This marked the beginning of their journey towards becoming one of the most successful R&B groups in history, known as The Isley Brothers.
