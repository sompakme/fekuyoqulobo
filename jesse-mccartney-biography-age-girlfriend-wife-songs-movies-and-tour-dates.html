Jesse Mccartney Biography

New York born Jesse McCartney is a singer, songwriter, and actor. He rose to fame in the late 1990s on the daytime drama All My Children as JR Chandler. Later he joined boy band Dream Street, and after sometime he branched out into a solo musical career. In addition he has appeared on shows such as Law & Order: SVU, Summerland, and Greek.

Jesse is also best known for lending his voice as Theodore in Alvin and the Chipmunks and its sequels, as well as voicing Robin/Nightwing in Young Justice and Roxas and Ventus in the video game series Kingdom Hearts developed by Square Enix.

Jesse Mccartney Family

McCartney was born, in Ardsley, Westchester, New York.  He is  the son of Ginger (Sarber) and Scott McCartney. When he was seven years old he began performing in local community musicals, before joining the national tour of The King and I at age ten along with Phil of the Future star Ricky Ullman. There Jesse played the character of Louis.  He sang with the group Sugar Beats in 1998,and can be heard on their 1998, 1999, and 2000 CD releases.

Jesse Mccartney Have It All

The studio album, Have It All by Jesse McCartney  was released on December 28, 2010.  It was released by Hollywood Records as McCartney’s fourth studio album.

Jesse Mccartney Dream Street

Jesse joined the American pop boy band Dream Street in 1999 ,and was a member until 2002. He has described the experience as a good “stepping stone” for his solo career. The group earned a gold record with their debut CDBrother

Jesse Mccartney Beautiful Soul Album

In July 2003 Jesse released his first solo EP . It featured three songs: “Beautiful Soul”, “Don’t You”, “Why Don’t You Kiss Her”. He performed a duet with Anne Hathaway, “Don’t Go Breaking My Heart” in 2004, which is featured on the Ella Enchanted soundtrack.

Jesse Mccartney Disney

Jesse  has contributed to numerous Disney soundtracks including Disneymania 2, Radio Disney Jingle Jams, A Cinderella Story, That’s So Raven, Stuck in the Suburbs, Lizzie McGuire Total Party!, Disneymania 3, Sydney White, Radio Disney Jams, Vol. 7, Radio Disney Jams, Vol. 8, DisneyMania 4, Hannah Montana, Radio Disney: Party Jams, That’s So Raven Too!, Radio Disney Jams, Vol. 9, Radio Disney Jams, Vol. 11, and The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement.

McCartney also appeared on the Disney Channel teen sitcoms The Suite Life of Zack & Cody and Hannah Montana. He also voices ‘Terence’ in the Tinker Bell franchise.

Jesse Mccartney All My Children

Jesse played Adam Chandler, Jr. in the ABC soap opera All My Children from 1998–2001, a role for which he earned two Young Artist Awards and two Daytime Emmy Award nominations.

Jesse Mccartney Keith

Jesse co-starred along with Elisabeth Harnois in an independent teenage drama feature film, Keith in 2008, directed by Todd Kessler. It is his movie debut, and features McCartney in the title role. Keith was released on September 19, 2008.

Jesse Mccartney Departure

On May 20, 2008 Jesse released his third album, Departure,  in the United States and Canada. Musically, it is a departure from his early works, showcasing more mature themes. The album has reached number 14 on the Billboard 200

Jesse Mccartney Summerland

Jesse  starred in the short-lived series Summerland, which aired on The WB for two seasons, playing orphaned teenager Bradin Westerly.

Jesse Mccartney Kingdom Hearts

Jesse worked in several installments of the Square Enix video game series Kingdom Hearts. In 2006 he was featured as Roxas in Kingdom Hearts II, and reprised the role in 2009 in Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days
