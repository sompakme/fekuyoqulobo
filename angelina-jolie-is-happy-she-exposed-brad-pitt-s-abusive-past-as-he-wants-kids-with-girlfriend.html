Angelina Apparently Has No Regrets Exposing Brad’s Alleged Abuse

While Angelina apparently recorded some of her and Brad’s argument before she left him, she was apprehensive to use them as evidence for fear of how it’d portray both of them. But as it appears she’s pulling out the last of her reserves as she aims to maintain primary custody of their minor kids, a new report says Angelina has no regrets.

“Angie is glad the truth is finally out there,” an insider told Life & Style . “Brad, on the other hand, is at his wit’s end, telling friends that this nightmare never ends. It just keeps getting worse.”

There have been claims that Brad is willing to accept a custody agreement that’s less than ideal, considering how long they’ve been arguing. And if Angelina is going to submit recordings proving her allegations, he may have even more incentive to agree.

Evidence of this includes a leaked Instagram post written on a past Father’s Day by his son, Pax, which called Brad out for allegedly being an absence dad. His oldest daughter, Zahara, also appeared to have dropped his surname and is just going by Angelina’s last name, Pitt, while attending college.

Brad Reportedly Wants Kids With His Girlfriend Despite His Ongoing Custody Battle

The actor appears to be thinking about the future, especially when it comes to his relationship with Ines De Ramon, who’s nearly 30 years older than her. Reports say Pitt wants to have children with Ines as well as settle down.

“Brad and Ines are smitten with each other and she recently moved into his Los Feliz mansion, proving how serious they are,” an insider claimed to Closer Magazine. “He’s known from the outset that Ines wants to become a mum and that’s something they discussed very early on, given their 30-year age gap.”
