Camila Cabello Sells Her Sprawling Hollywood Hills Mansion For $4.3 Million

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Camila paid $3.4 million for the four-bedroom, four-bathroom house in April 2019 and chose to put the stunning home, which is located above Sunset Strip, on the auction early this year for $3.95 million. Camila consented to a sale last month, immediately after announcing her breakup from Shawn Mendes. The 24-year-old famous pop star paid $3.4 million for the house in April 2019 and hoped to make a $500K profit if she achieved her list price.

The stunning four-bedroom house has a beautiful location above Sunset Strip. The singer hoped to make an extra profit of $500K.

Although her first valuation would just have netted her a profit of more than $500,000, the deal she recently completed brings her a whopping profit of $900,000. Camila Cabello's gorgeous four-bedroom, four-bathroom property situated above the Sunset Strip in Los Angeles has found a buyer for $3.95 million. Denise Rosner, a Compass agent, advertised the residence, which the performer had indeed evacuated.

Meanwhile, Scott Moore of Douglas Ellman represented the buyer. The 3,570-square-foot house is situated on a 6,320-square-foot (0.15-acre) plot. The Moroccan renaissance property features a private courtyard entry and a partitioned open floor layout with weathered wood flooring combined with import tiling. The house has a modern kitchen accented with stainless steel equipment and stone surfaces, as well as a breakfast bar that links to the dining area, according to Mansion Global.

The lounge room has a Spanish tiles fireplace and an additional lounge space, while the two above levels include three en-suite rooms. The master bedroom has a fireplace and a huge walk-in closet, whereas the big master retreat also has a fireplace and a huge walk-in closet. Cabello transformed the fourth bedroom into a state-of-the-art recording studio inside the adjacent two-car garage, complete with a professional vocal booth.

The outside amenities include a multi-level deck with a saline pool, an outdoor terrace eating area, several lounging places, a wood-burning fireplace, and a recently constructed BBQ area. According to Daily Mail, the house also has an additional security system, which was built when Cabello's ex-boyfriend, singer Shawn, had his new G-Wagon stolen from the driveway when they both were at home in March.

 

Mr. Moore stated that the buyer visited 40 to 50 houses before settling on Ms. Cabello's. "Of most of the places we looked at, well, this spoke with us the strongest," he added. "In comparison to everything else we viewed, the character, charm, and vitality of the residence stole the show here."
