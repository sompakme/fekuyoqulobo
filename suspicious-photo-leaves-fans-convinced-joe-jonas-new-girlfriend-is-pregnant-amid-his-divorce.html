Joe Jonas still hasn’t finalized his divorce from Sophie Turner, even though they’ve both moved on in new relationships. While the musician’s romance with Stormi Bree is new, some fans are convinced the couple are about to reach a new milestone – parenthood. A recent photo of Stormy has sparked debate over whether she has a baby bump.

Earlier this week, Stormi, 33, posted a photo to Instagram but was quick to delete it, leading fans to question why she was so eager to get it off the Internet.

Why Fans Think Joe’s Girlfriend Stormi Bree Is Pregnant

In the photo, Stormi’s bare stomach was visible as she held her hands above her head, causing her cropped t-shirt to ride up. The social media personality styled her look with corduroy overalls and a slouchy bag. Stormi posed under a tree while looking off into the corner, but fans couldn’t take their eyes off her midsection.

Though only a sliver of her stomach was visible, some fans thought they saw a baby bump, which would explain why Stormi was quick to delete it, since she hasn’t made any formal announcement.

The photo kicked up a storm on Reddit, where many people questioned what they saw . "Is Stormi Bree pregnant with Joe Jonas’ baby? A new baby Jonas coming soon," one person wrote alongside a screenshot of the now-deleted photo.

"That sure does look like a very pregnant belly button to me," one comment said, with another echoing a similar sentiment, "Woah it takes a min for your belly button to pop out like that too..."

"Wait I noticed this too but wasn't sure if I was being delulu," another added.

Joe Allegedly Hasn’t Introduced His Girlfriend To His Kids

Stormi and Joe haven’t been connected romantically for very long. They were first linked in January, a few months after his ex Sophie was spotted with British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson. Joe was the one who filed for divorce in September.

Joe and Sophie have been married since 2019 and they share two daughters. The Game of Thrones actress accused her estranged husband of withholding their daughters’ passports, tantamount to kidnapping, in a lawsuit she launched after his divorce filing.
