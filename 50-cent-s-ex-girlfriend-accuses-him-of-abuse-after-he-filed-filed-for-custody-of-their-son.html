50 Cent's Ex-Girlfriend Accuses Him Of Abuse After He Filed Filed For Custody Of Their Son

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Highlights

  • Daphne Joy's name-drop in a lawsuit led to 50 Cent seeking full custody of their son, sparking a public dispute on social media.
  • Daphne Joy accused 50 Cent of rape and abuse, claiming he was an absentee father who caused damage to the family.
  • In response, 50 Cent denied the allegations, stating his main focus is to protect his son by seeking sole custody.

The lives of 50 Cent and his ex, Daphne Joy, were heavily impacted when she was name-dropped in the lawsuit against Diddy Combs. While she wasn't accused in the lawsuit, her involvement caused the rapper to want to pursue full custody of their twelve-year-old son, Sire.

Throughout his career, there have been many women who have fallen into 50 Cent's arms, including singer Ciara and American socialite Paris Hilton.

However, as soon as the news of his decision was made public, Daphne Joy slammed him on her Instagram, accusing him of raping and physically abusing her during their time together. She also alleged that he barely saw his son, and that he caused "permanent damage" to the father-son relationship.

50 Cent's Ex Accused Him Of Rape

50 Cent and Daphne Joy weren't involved for too long, and split shortly after having a son. While not much was said of their relationship at the time, now Daphne Joy is speaking out, accusing the rapper of rape and abuse during their relationship.

"Let's put the real focus on your true evil actions of raping me and physically abusing me," she wrote on her Instagram. "You are no longer my oppressor and my God will handle you from this point on. You have permanently damaged the last hope I had for you as a father to preserve our family with these last and final false claims made against me. You have broken our hearts for the last and final time."

She also accused the rapper of being an absentee father and not caring about his son.

"We moved to New York to give you the opportunity to be a father to your son and you saw him 10 times out of the 2 years that we lived 1 mile away from you. I am tired of upholding and protecting an image to our son that you have never even earned."

50 Cent Denied The Allegations And Is Seeking Full Custody Of His Son

Immediately after Daphne Joy made those accusations against 50 Cent, the rapper issued a statement denying any wrongdoing and anticipating that he would take legal action.

"The disturbing allegations in the sworn pleadings recently filed in a court case related to Daphne Joy the mother of my twelve year old child, has required me to take all necessary legal actions to protect my son Sire," 50 Cent said to VIBE. "The most recent false and baseless accusations by Daphne Joy are clearly in response to my decision to seek sole custody of my son. My son Sire is my main priority and keeping him in a safe environment is my only focus at this time."
