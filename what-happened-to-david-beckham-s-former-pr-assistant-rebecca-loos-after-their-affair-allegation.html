Who Is Rebecca Loos?

According to The Sun, Rebecca Loos was born in Madrid, Spain but soon became a Dutch citizen due to her diplomat father, Leonard Loos Bartholdi. She is also a second cousin of journalist and personality Piers Morgan.

While we don't know what Loos' career aspirations were when she was young, she would soon go on to take on numerous roles in the public eye. But before all of that, she worked as David Beckham's personal assistant following his transfer to the Real Madrid football club in 2003.

What Happened Between David Beckham And Rebecca Loos?

Rebecca Loos only worked as David Beckham's personal assistant for a short period of time. Her very public allegations of having an affair with the famed athlete during a four-month period in Madrid, Spain put an end to their working relationship.

According to The Sun, Rebecca Loos gave an interview to News of the World (a British tabloid) in April 2004. This is where she alleged that she had been sleeping with Beckham even though he was married to the former Spice Girl. However, Loos offered no concrete evidence that such an affair ever took place.

Additionally, David Beckham called the allegations "ludicrous" and vehemently denied that anything of the sport ever happened. And while Victoria Beckham has said that she has worked hard to keep her marriage alive, she too stood by his side and denied the allegations.

The pair also stood by each other when he was accused of having an affair with model Sarah Marbeck and with beautician Danielle Heath. All of the accusations surfaced around the same time as Loos's interview with News of the World.
