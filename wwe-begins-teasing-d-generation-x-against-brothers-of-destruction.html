WWE Begins Teasing D-Generation X Against Brothers Of Destruction

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Triple H and the Undertaker are set to go one-on-one next weekend at WWE Super Show-Down in Australia, but they won’t be doing it alone. “The Game” will be accompanied by his mentor Shawn Michaels, while “The Deadman” will be joined by his brother Kane. It’s being billed as the final chapter of the longtime rivalry, but things are rarely that simple in the world of professional wrestling.

Reports have surfaced over the past month that Shawn Michaels is coming out of retirement, and even the television product has been teasing another match between Michaels and Undertaker. So, with all the ingredients being there in Australia, many expect for a tag team match between the four to be made for WWE Crown Jewel. Even WWE is teasing the idea now, as their Instagram page asks who would win between the two teams.
