Usher And Jennifer Began Dating In 2019

Usher has always been quite public when it comes to his romances, so his relationship with his current girlfriend, Jennifer Goicoechea was certainly no exception. The couple reportedly began dating back in October of 2019 and have been together ever since. The singer was previously married not once, but twice to both Tameka Foster and Grace Harry, however, fans feel as if his romance with Jenn is unlike any of his others.

The duo took things to the next level quite quickly into their relationship, announcing that Jennifer was pregnant with their first child together in early 2020. Come September 30 of that year, the duo welcomed a baby girl, Sovereign Bo Raymond. "We are feeling blessed and full of love with the arrival of our beautiful baby girl," Usher wrote on his Instagram.

She Is A Music Executive At Epic Records

While we know Jennifer as Usher's girlfriend and baby mama, she's quite known within the music industry. Jennifer Goicoechea was the director of the rhythm and soul department at ASCAP, which is a non-profit organization that works to protect its client's musical copyrights.

In addition to her former role at ASCAP, Goicoechea is now the Vice President of A&R at Epic Records, which is quite major! The label represents massive names in the music biz including Travis Scott, 21 Savage, Rick Ross, Mariah Carey, and Meghan Trainor, to name a few.
