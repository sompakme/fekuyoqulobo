    Career in Television World

    Niyati Fatnani is a well-known Indian actress, model, and dancer. In 2016, she made her acting debut with D4 – Get Up and Dance where she played the role of Niharika ‘Baby’ Sinha alongside actor Utkarsh Gupta.

    In the same year, she played the role of Shazia in an episode of Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya. In 2017, Niyati portrayed the role of Aru in the tv serial Yeh Moh Moh Ke Dhaagey alongside Eijaz Khan. 

    From 2018 to 2021, Fatnani played the main role of Piya Rathod / Piya Sharma in 428 episodes of Nazar opposite Harsh Rajput which proved as a major turning point in her career.

    In 2021, she also played the character of Piya in the telefilm Ankahee Dastaan. In 2022, Niyati played Ginni Grewal / Ginni Aditya Singh in Channa Mereya opposite actor Karan Wahi.

    In the same year, she made her guest appearance on the reality show Swayamvar – Mika Di Vohti. In 2023, she is in the limelight for her appearance as Asmita Roy in the tv series Dear Ishq premiered on 26 January 2023 on Disney+ Hotstar.

    In this series, she is seen along with actor Sehban Azim. Currently, Fatnani is also filming for her upcoming serial Ishq Mein Ghayal where she will be seen playing the role of Avantika.
