American television personality, Ronald "Ronnie" Ortiz-Mgro, Jr., has an estimated net worth of $1.8 million. He first came to prominence as one of the eight main cast members in the MTV reality series Jersey Shore, earning $100,000 per episode as of season 4. He started dating his current on/off girlfriend Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola, a fellow castmate on the reality TV show, in early August 2009. Ortiz-Magro is also known for being the Xenadrine vitamin sponsor in their commercials and recently started his own clothing line “Jersey Laundry.”
