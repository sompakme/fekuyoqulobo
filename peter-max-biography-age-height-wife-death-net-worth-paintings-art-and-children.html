Who is Peter Max? Peter Max Biography and Wiki

Peter Max is a German-American artist known for using bright colors in his work. Works by Max are associated with the visual arts and culture of the 1960s, particularly psychedelic art and pop art.

Peter Max Age and Birthday

Peter Max was born Peter Max Finkelstein on October 19, 1937, in Berlin, Germany. He celebrates his birthday on October 19th every year. Max will be 83 years old on Oct. 19th, 2020.

Peter Max Height and Weight

Max is a man of average stature, he also appears to be quite tall in stature in his photos. He stands at a height of 5 feet and 9 inches (1.75 meters). He also weighs 160 lbs (73 kgs).

Peter Max Education

Max studied at Lafayette High School. He also attended the Art Students League of New York. In 1956, Max began his formal art training at the Art Students League of New York in Manhattan, studying anatomy, figure drawing, and composition under Frank J. Reilly who had studied at the League alongside Norman Rockwell.

Peter Max Parents and Siblings

Peter was born to his parents Jacob Finkelstein (Father) and Salla Finkelstein (Mother). After doing our research, details about his family are not known to the public and it is also not known if he has any siblings.

Max was born in Berlin, the son of German Jews, Sala, and Jacob. They fled Berlin in 1938, settling in Shanghai, China, where they lived for the next ten years. In 1948, the family moved to Haifa, Israel, where they lived for several years. From Israel, the family continued moving westward and stopped in Paris for several months—an experience that Max said greatly influenced his appreciation for art—eventually settling in Brooklyn, New York City

He was also romantically connected with Tina Louise. Max married Mary Balkin in 1997; she died by suicide (nitrogen asphyxiation) in June 2019.

Peter Max Career

Max began his art career in 1962 when he launched a small arts studio in Manhattan called ‘The Daly & Max Studio’ alongside his friend Tom Daly. In 1968, he appeared on The Tonight Show and he was also featured on Life Magazine.

In 1970, his works were featured in the exhibition titled ‘The World of Peter Max’. He has been the official artist for many major events like the 1994 World Cup, the Grammy Awards, the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, the super bowls, and many more. He was also the official artist of the 2000 World Series.

In 1989, he designed the cover photo of Aretha Franklin’s Through the Storm album. In 1994, he designed the artwork for the progressive rock band Yes’s fourteenth studio album Talk. Over the years, Max’s artworks have been associated with the counter culture, neo-expressionism, and psychedelic movements.

Max’s artwork was featured on CBS’s The Early Show where his ’44 Obamas’ commemorating Barack Obama, the 44th president of the United States was debuted. In 2013, a book titled The Universe of Peter was published by HarperCollins.

Peter Max Painting

Max is best known for painting, drawing, etchings, collage, printmaking, sculpture, video and digital imagery. He often uses American icons and symbols in his artwork. He has created paintings of Ford, Carter, Reagan, and Bush. He regularly features images of celebrities, politicians, athletes and sporting events, and other pop culture subjects in his artwork. He is known for using bursts of color which often contains much or all of the visible spectrum.

Peter Max Etchings

Max works in multiple media including painting, drawing, etchings (including aquatint), collage, printmaking, sculpture, video, and digital imagery. He also includes “mass media” as being another “canvas” for his creative expression
