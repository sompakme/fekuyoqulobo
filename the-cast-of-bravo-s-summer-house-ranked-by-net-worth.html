The Cast Of Bravo's 'Summer House' Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-31

When it comes to Bravo, the network has a plethora of reality shows fans can enjoy! Whether you are a hardcore fan of Real Housewives, Southern Charm, or Below Deck, there is no denying the drama some of our Bravo favs bring. Well, on top of table flipping, yachting, and hitting the races, Bravo's Summer House also brings the heat each and every season.

The show first came about back in 2017 and is currently airing its fifth season! Fans were introduced to the likes of Carl Radke, Kyle Cooke, and Lindsay Hubbard, all of whom have become favorites on the show. The hit series also found itself having a crossover moment when the cast of Vanderpump Rules paid a visit to our Summer House friends in Montauk, New York.

Considering the cast rents out a house in the Hamptons area every summer, things can definitely get expensive, especially with this group! While we know current cast member Kyle Cooke has the highest net worth of the bunch, just how much is the entire cast worth?

10 Lauren & Ashley Wirkus - $1.4 Million

Ashley and Lauren Wirkus joined the series during its start back in 2017 and definitely brought the heat! The duo, who were referred to as "double trouble" knew exactly how to spend their summer the right way.

While both Wirkus twins have since left the show, they have gone on to focus on parenthood and their respective careers. Considering the two are quite the hard-working pair, it is no surprise that Lauren has managed to earn an estimated net worth of $600,000, while sister, Ashely has a net worth of $800,000, bringing their combined net worth to $1.4 million!

9 Everett Watson - $1.4 Million

Everett Scott Watson made his reality television debut during the very first season of Summer House. The star was dating fellow cast member, Lindsay Hubbard at the time, however, after a number of relationship mess-ups, the duo officially called it quits.

Everett made a number of appearances come the second season, however, that would be the last fans saw of him. Watson has since returned back to his job in New York City where he works in financial risk management, earning himself an estimated net worth of $1.4 million. 

8 Kyle Cooke - $1.1 Million

Kyle Cooke is without a doubt the fan-favorite when it comes to the Summer House cast! While his wild and messy ways may make for great reality television, it certainly hasn't done wonders for his relationship with Amanda Betula. Luckily for Kyle, not only is his relationship in great shape, but his professional life couldn't be better either!

The star launched his beverage and lifestyle brand, Loverboy, in 2019, which has exploded, particularly amongst Bravo fans. Now, with the success of his business and the show, Cooke has managed to earn himself a net worth of $1.1 million. 

7 Lindsay Hubbard - $1.1 Million

Lindsay Hubbard had a rough start on Summer House back in season one when she and ex-boyfriend, Everett Watson had quite the rollercoaster ride of a summer. Despite the duo having split up, they remain "friendly" to this day, however, in recent seasons, it appears as if Lindsay has eyes for fellow housemate, Carl Radke.

While she's single and definitely ready to mingle, Lindsay is also one of the hardest working cast members. The star is a PR mogul in New York City, and luckily for her, running her own firm has led to her amassing a net worth of $1.1 million. 

6 Paige DeSorbo - $ 1 Million

Paige DeSorbo joined the cast of Bravo's Summer House during its third season alongside Hannah Brenner. The two replaced Danielle Olivera and Jordan Verrois and have since remained on board full-time.

As for DeSorbo, the star reigns on social media and has a massive following. In addition to being a reality personality, the star takes on a number of jobs from endorsement and brand deals, all of which have allowed her to amass an impressive net worth of $1 million. 

5 Danielle Olivera - $1 Million

Danielle Olivera joined the show in its second season, however, she left after the third and has yet to make a return. The star works as a project manager in New York City with a focus on driving user engagement and revenue "through big data, design, and automation," says TV Over Mind. While she may not have lasted long on the show, she remains a success in her own industry, amassing a net worth of $1 million.

4 Carl Radke - $500,000

Carl Radke has been the Summer House heartthrob ever since his appearance back in season one. As an original cast member, Carl has become a fan favorite alongside bestie, Kyle Cooke, and rightfully so!

While he has made a nice chunk of cash from the show, Carl works in sales back in the Big Apple and has also partnered with Cooke and fiancee, Amanda Batula in their brand, Loverboy. As for Radke, his work has led him to earn an estimated $500,000. 

3 Hannah Berner - $500,000

Hannah Berner joined the cast alongside Paige DeSorbo back in season three and has proven herself to be quite the TV personality. Prior to joining the cast, Berner worked in media as a video producer for Betches.com along with co-hosting Bravo's podcast, Chat Room, and her very own podcast, Berning In Hell.

Luckily, her success in media has not only allowed Hannah to make a name for herself, but it has also allowed her to amass an estimated net worth of $500,000. 

2 Luke Gulbranson - $200,000

While Luke Gulbranson may not have been a full-time cast member, his relationship with Hannah Berner definitely got him some screen-time. Although he may not have been fit for reality television, Luke definitely has an act for acting.

The star recently had a cameo in HBO Max'sThe Flight Attendant, but he won't be quitting his day job as a jeweler anytime soon. Gulbranson takes pride in his designs, which he boasts about on Instagram, and it appears to have done right by him as he is worth an estimated $200,000. 

1 Amanda Batula - $100,000

Amanda Betula joined the series as a full-time cast member come the second season, however, she definitely felt the wrath of infidelity and lies during the first. While her relationship with Kyle Cooke has certainly come a long way, the duo continue to learn and grow from their past selves.

Despite the two having to postpone their wedding due to Covid-19, they are keeping an optimistic outlook and putting their focus all into Loverboy, which Amanda is also a partner of. Due to her success on the show and with her company, Amanda has amassed a net worth of $100,000.
