Joe E. Brown Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Joseph Evans Brown (July 28, 1891 – July 6, 1973) was an American actor and comedian who is most known for his affable film demeanour, quick wit, and infectious grin on screen. He was one of the most popular American comedians throughout the 1930s and 1940s, thanks to films such as A Midsummer Night’s Dream, Earthworm Tractors, and Alibi Ike, which were all hits at the box office. In his latter career, Brown appeared as Osgood Fielding III in the film Some Like It Hot, in which he delivered the famous quip “Well, nobody’s perfect.”
