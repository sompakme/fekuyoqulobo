Who Was Cara Williams Ex-Husband Asher Dann?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Cara Williams was born on June 29, 1925, in Brooklyn, New York, the US and her parents were Romanian, Jewish immigrants, and Benjamin Irving Kamiat, a Jewish immigrant born in Lemberg, Austrian Empire. She loved to watch the movies of all the stars on the screens and at the time, she wanted to be an actress.

The actress was caught with the acting bug when she was a small child. Her mother worked near Brooklyn’s Albee Theatre and used to leave her there with the theatre owners. She grew up watching movies there and became interested in acting.

Who Was Cara Williams’ Ex-Husband Asher Dann?

Asher Dann was born on May 5, 1934. He was an actor, known for The Investigators (1961), The Case of the Dangerous Robin (1960), and September Storm (1960). He was married to Cara Williams. He died on March 18, 2018.
