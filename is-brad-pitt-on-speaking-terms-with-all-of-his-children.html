Is Brad Pitt On Speaking Terms With All Of His Children?

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Highlights

  • Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's divorce was prompted by an alleged altercation on their private jet and has resulted in years of legal battles and custody disputes.
  • Brad's relationship with his children is complicated, with his eldest sons, Maddox and Pax, reportedly not speaking to him, while he remains actively involved in his younger kids' lives.
  • Brad does not force his children to see him and supports their decisions, spending low-key, private time with them at home.

For over a decade, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie were one of the biggest couples in Hollywood. Together, they built a large family with six children -- Maddox, 22, Pax, 20, Zahara, 18, Shiloh, 17, and twins Knox and Vivienne, 15. But only two years after legally tying the knot, Angelina surprised fans by filing for divorce. And so ensued years of legal battles, even until today, despite their messy Hollywood divorce being finalized years ago.

The catalyst for Brad and Angelina’s divorce was a simple one for Angelina. The divorce occurred after an alleged altercation that took place on their private jet in front of their kids in 2016.

Initially, Angelina was granted physical custody of their six kids, while Brad only had visitation rights. He was given joint custody in May 2021. However, Angelina later won an appeal against the joint custody. She currently retains sole custody, while Brad has visitation of their minor children.

Given the complicated family dynamic, many fans wonder how close Brad is to his kids. Here’s what we know about their relationship.

Brad Pitt’s Oldest Kids Were Refusing To Speak To Him, According To Rumors

Maddox Jolie Is The Only Child Not Talking To Brad Pitt These Days

Brad’s relationship with his children has been complicated following his divorce from Angelina. In 2020, Us Weekly reported that his eldest sons, Maddox and Pax, weren’t speaking to him following the plane altercation that happened years ago. However, Brad reportedly remained active in his younger kids’ lives.

Case in point: according to a source who spoke with Us Weekly, "He flew to Italy for the twins’ birthday and that meant a lot to them, plus he made a big fuss of Shiloh on her 16th."

It certainly seems, then, as though Brad is still on good terms with his younger kids, and things have improved as they’ve gotten older.

"Brad has dinner with his younger kids when they are all in L.A.," a source explained. "Since the kids are older now, they have their own life and friends. But, Brad still has a pretty good relationship with them."

It appeared that, except for Maddox, the relationship between the older kids was on its way to being mended as well. That is until Pax seemingly slammed his father for being an absentee dad in a secret Instagram post.

But with that said, when Zahara was introducing herself to her sorority, she introduced herself as "Zahara Jolie." The Pitt had been dropped from her name, something that Maddox, and according to some sources, Shiloh had already done in the past. Whether that is a signal of more of an alliance to Angelina than Brad remains to be seen. However, it speaks volumes to just how close Brad is with some of his children that they have made the decision as they have gotten older to take the hyphenate out of their name.

Brad Pitt Allegedly Doesn’t Force His Kids To See Him

Even After Winning Joint Custody, Brad Pitt Did Not Make His Kids Have Visitation Time

Brad has spent years fighting Angelina in court for joint custody, and he continues to. But reports say that the actor doesn’t force his children to do anything against his will, so visiting him likely isn’t mandatory if the kids object.

"He supports his kids and what makes them happy," a source told E! News in 2021.

"They are old enough now where they can make decisions and he doesn't stand in the way. He's encouraged them to be whoever they want to be. He wouldn't ask for anything different."

Brad Pitt's Relationship With His Kids Briefly Soured After A Private Jet Incident

Brad Pitt Was Reportedly Under The Influence When He Fought With Angelina Jolie On The Jet

It’s unclear how close Brad is to his six children nowadays. It’s also unclear how close he was with them before the divorce. Brad’s parenting came under fire after Angelina left him. Not only was she granted custody, but Brad was investigated for foul play. The Los Angeles' Department of Children and Family Services later cleared him of the charges.

With this in mind, it’s possible that the kids’ relationship with Brad was slightly strained before his marital strife with Angelina was publicized.

He does have visitation rights and coupled with reports from sources who say he’s on good terms with his younger kids, Brad appears active to an extent with some of his children. But this likely can’t be said about his eldest.

His court-ordered visitation can’t be enforced on any of his children who are no longer minors. Right now, this includes his eldest sons Maddox, 22, and Pax, 20, who he’s reportedly had the most tenuous relationship. Daughter Zahara is now also 18.

Though Brad is currently entitled to time with his younger kids, it won’t be long before they start aging out of the visitation order. His second daughter, Shiloh, has just one year to go.
