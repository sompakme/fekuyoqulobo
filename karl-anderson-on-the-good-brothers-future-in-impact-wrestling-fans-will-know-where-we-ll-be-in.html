Karl Anderson On The Good Brothers Future In IMPACT Wrestling: Fans Will Know Where Well Be In

Publish date: 2024-09-24

Never say never in the wrestling business.

Former IMPACT Tag Team Champions Karl Anderson, alongside his tag partner Doc Gallows spoke on their Talk n’ Shop podcast, and the pair discussed their future in IMPACT Wrestling and their plans for free agency.

“IMPACT took a chance on us in the middle of one of the most insane times in the history of our life,” said Anderson. “They took a chance on us and so I’ll love IMPACT forever. I love AEW’s office, I love their boss, I love their bosses, I love their people, I love IMPACT’s bosses, I love their people and in about a year, you’ll find out exactly where the Good Brothers are gonna be.”

Anderson also briefly mentioned how he has no intentions on doing a Talk n’ Shop-A-Mania 3.

“One thing I’m for sure not doing is Talk n’ Shop-A-Mania 3!” said Anderson.
