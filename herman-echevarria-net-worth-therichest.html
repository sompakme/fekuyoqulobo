American businessman Herman Echevarria has an estimated net worth of $30 million. He is the chairman and CEO of BVK/Meka, a full-service international marketing and communications firm based in Miami and Milwaukee. It has more than 220 professionals in 12 offices in the United States. It also develops integrated communications campaigns with focus on the U.S. General Market, U.S. Hispanic and the Latin American markets.

A Cuban-born multimillionaire, Echevarria took over in 2002 as the chairman of the Miami-Dade 2000 Marketing Initiative. It was a marketing initiative launched by the Beacon Council that promotes Miami-Dade County as a premier business destination through pro-active and targeted marketing campaigns. Also, he has been responsible in raising over $3 million for such campaigns from the private and public sectors. It is expected to be around $9 million in the next three years.

With his accomplishments and achievements in the business world, Herman Echevarria was recognized by the Miami Business Review in 2001 as one of the top 100 most influential leaders in South Florida. Aside from business, he is also known as the husband of Alexia Echevarria, one of the stars of The Real Housewives of Miami.
