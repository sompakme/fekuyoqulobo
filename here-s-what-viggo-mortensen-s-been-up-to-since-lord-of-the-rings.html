Here's What Viggo Mortensen's Been Up To Since 'Lord Of The Rings'

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Lord of the Rings remains one of the most incredible film franchises of all time, and even now, people are still watching these films on repeat. Many facts have emerged about this incredible trilogy, and the actors that had lead roles in the film have been reaping the rewards of their hard work for years.

Viggo Mortensen played Aragorn in the trilogy, cementing himself as one of the best actors in the game with his incredible performance in each film. Naturally, he has amassed an incredible net worth, and he has built an amazing legacy for himself in the business. After all this time, Viggo continues to work and wow audiences with his acting abilities.

So, what has the acclaimed actor been up to since appearing in Lord of the Rings? Let’s take a look at just how busy he has been!

Academy Award Nominations

Any actor as good as Viggo Mortensen is sure to please fans and critics alike with stellar performances, but it is rare to see someone nab multiple Oscar nominations. For Viggo, his work since Lord of the Rings has been instrumental in keeping him at the top of the pile.

Back in 2007, just a few years after his time in the trilogy was up, Viggo would land a role in the film Eastern Promises, which was an amazing movie. It was a stark contrast from playing Aragorn, and he wound up delivering a performance for the ages. He would be nominated for an Academy Award for Best Actor, which would be the first of his career.

Despite having good performances in other projects, it would be some time before Viggo would find himself nominated for another Oscar. In 2017, Viggo was once again in awards contention thanks to his memorable performance in the film Captain Fantastic, according to IMDb. Once again, he would leave the ceremony without the award, but getting his second nomination must have felt incredible.

Despite not winning either Oscar, the actor was still doing quite well for himself. Just two years later, Viggo would take part in a movie that went on to surprise a lot of people. Not only would he find himself up for some hardware, but the film itself made waves as it did better than most were anticipating.

Best Picture Oscar

After years of churning out solid performances, Viggo Mortensen was 0 for 2 at the Oscars. Despite this, he was still doing his thing and making waves. In 2019, things seemed like they might change for the performer after starring in the film Green Book.

The movie itself earned some incredible reviews from people, and it was nominated for the Best Picture Oscar, according to IMDb. Incredibly, Viggo would also receive another nomination for his performance in the film. While the films had some hype, no one was anticipating what happened next.

The film took home the Academy Award for Best Picture, completely shocking the entertainment industry. While it was a great film that had a lot to like about it, many people felt that it would go down as one of the least memorable Best Picture winners in history. Nevertheless, all the hard work that the cast and crew put into making the movie clearly paid off in the end.

Mortensen, despite doing his thing in the movie, once again left the ceremony without an Oscar to his name. Despite missing out on the award, it was still a huge night for him, as his film took home the biggest award of the evening. Not only that, but his third nomination cemented him as one of the best performers of his era.

Viggo was on a roll, but his fans were always wanting to see him circle back to Middle Earth. Turns out, Josh Gad had just the right idea to get the Fellowship back together.

One Reunion To Rule Them All

Having been away from Middle Earth for years and going on to achieve greatness, things could not have been going better for Viggo Mortensen. However, the Horn of Gondor must have been blowing earlier this year, as Viggo got back together with the Fellowship!

Josh Gad’s YouTube channel had a brilliant idea to reunite casts from some of the best movies in recent memory, and when it was announced that the Lord of the Rings cast was on board, fans were patiently waiting to see how things would play out.

Thankfully, their reunion episode was nothing short of amazing, and seeing Viggo with the rest of the cast made all of those that grew up with the trilogy feel young again. The cast had a chance to reminisce and tell interesting stories, and it really showed how much chemistry they still have after all these years.
