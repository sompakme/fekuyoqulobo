Caroline Ellison Net Worth: What Is Caroline Ellison Net Worth?

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Caroline Ellison has an estimated net worth of $15 million. She accrued this wealth through her profession.

Ellison is an American former business executive. She is the former CEO of Alameda Research, a quantitative trading firm founded by Sam Bankman-Fried.

Raised in Boston, Ellison is a 2016 graduate of Stanford University with a bachelor’s degree in mathematics.

After her graduation, she worked for the quantitative trading firm Jane Street. Ellison served as a junior trader at the company for 19 months.

In March 2018, Ellison joined Alameda Research and became co-CEO along with Sam Trabucco in October 2021. She became the sole CEO of the company in August 2022 after Trabucco stepped down.

After CoinDesk raised concerns about the balance sheet of Alameda Research and its relationship with FTX on 6 November 2022, Ellison said that the balance sheet information which had been released only included some of Alameda’s assets and that the firm had more than $10 billion of additional assets.

In December 2022, Ellison pleaded guilty to two counts of wire fraud, two counts of conspiracy to commit wire fraud, conspiracy to commit securities fraud, and conspiracy to commit money laundering.
