Swatted Meaning Explained

Swatting is a terrible thing that too many famous streamers and content creators do too often.

“Swatting” is the act of calling the police or another emergency service, usually under the guise of an immediate threat, to send a large number of armed police officers to the location of someone else.

This will happen if there is a false report of a serious law enforcement emergency, like a bomb threat, murder, or hostage situation, or a false report of a mental health emergency, like saying that a person is suicidal or planning to kill someone and may or may not have a gun.
