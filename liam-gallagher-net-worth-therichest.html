Liam Gallagher is a British born musician with a net worth of $60 million. Liam is the former lead singer for one of the most successful and popular British bands, Oasis. Currently, Gallagher is the lead singer for the band Beady Eye. Both bands have received critical acclaim in both Europe and internationally. Along with Liam, the band includes several original members of Oasis. Liam's voice has been compared to famed musicians John Lennon and John Lydon. Gallagher is easily recognized during concerts for his unique style of singing that includes him holding his hands behind his back. According to Gallagher, he does this to project additional power from his voice.

Gallagher is an easily recognized public figure that has angered many and excited others with his controversial comments. Gallagher has been quoted as making disparaging comments about Americans, Australians and religion. Despite the controversy, Liam Gallagher remains a popular British musician who is well-liked by many.
